about           = SwissSnow produces a 4-class avalanche-susceptibility map for an area of
                  interest in Switzerland. It combines morphological terrain factors (slope,
                  aspect, profile curvature) from the swissALTI3D 2 m DTM with a seasonal
                  snow-load factor from the Open-Meteo historical archive, plus optional
                  elevation snow-lapse and wind-loading corrections. A second tool validates
                  the result against SLF avalanche-accident data.
                  Target users: GIS analysts, natural-hazard and spatial-planning
                  professionals in Switzerland.
                  Not affiliated with SLF or swisstopo; not for operational safety decisions.
